Mike Watt

A man in a van in San Pedro

Here's a timeline of Mike Watt's life:

  • 1957 - Born in Portsmouth, Virginia
  • Mid 70s - Watt and D Boon form the Reactionaries with George Hurley
  • 1980 - The Minutemen sign with SST Records
  • 1984 - Watt meets Kira
  • 1985 - D Boon is killed in an automobile accident
  • 1986 - Watt starts hangin' with the Sonic Youth crew
  • 1986 - Watt meets Ed Crawford and form fIREHOSE with Hurley
  • 1987 - Watt marries Kira
  • 1993 - fIREHOSE breaks up
  • 1994 - Watt and Kira divorce
  • 1995 -Watt releases his first solo album, Ball-Hog or Tugboat?
  • 1996 - Watt plays on tour with Porno for Pyros
  • 2000 Watt gets seriously ill with an infection of his perineum
  • 2001 - The Watt from Pedro Show debuts
  • 2003 - Watt releases his first book
  • 2005 - McNally-Smith College of Music in Saint Paul, Minnesota announces the formation of the Mike Watt Bass Guitar Scholarship
  • 2006 - Watt records The Weirdness with The Stooges
  • 2006 - The Watt from Pedro Show becomes available as a podcast

    — Mike Watt
